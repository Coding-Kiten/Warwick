Warwick
Elementerary

Warwick Elementary is a elementary school that has 40 classrooms. It has a large cafeteria for basket ball games , talent shows, plays, and concerts .The playground is split in 3 sections. one is for kindergarten and pre-k. The second section of the playground is the primary section for grades 1-3. The last section is for inter mediate kids. Grades 4-6.

Events

Back to school night is the time when parents get informed on what the students are doing that year. The Hallow Ween Parade happens on Hallow Ween. All grades line up and circle the school.All the teachers dress up and the judges decide which grade of teachers wins the costume contest! 5 Basket Ball games occur each year in thec cafatiria. Other schools come to play these games. The talent show happens once a year. All students who want to participate will show off their talents to the judges. There are 3 winners.
